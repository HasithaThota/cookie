Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1cup of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/2 all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 2 cups ofchocolate chips(any kind your chose)

How to make step by step

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Then in a large bowl, mix together the butter,brown sugar and normal sugar until it's smooth.
  3. After beat in the eggs one at a time.
  4. Next combine flour,baking soda and and salt; stir into the sugar mixture.
  5. Then mix in the chocolate chips.
  6. After drop by drop tablespoonsfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes in the oven,or until the edges are golden.
  8. Finally remove cookies from tha baking sheet to cool on the wire racks.
  9. Enjoy!!!
